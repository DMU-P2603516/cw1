SYNOPSIS

Cecilia Kass is trapped in an abusive and violent relationship with a wealthy optics engineer and businessman named Adrian Griffin. One night, Cecilia drugs Adrian and makes her escape out of his highly secured home with the assistance of her sister, Emily.

Cecilia seeks shelter at the house of his acquaintance, Detective James Lanier and his teenage daughter, Sydney. Two weeks after Cecilia's escape, Adrian commits suicide and leaves Cecilia 5 million dollars in his trust. Adrian’s lawyer and also brother, Tom, handles the arrangements. Cecilia suspects another presence in the house after several strange, lethal and life-threatening events and calls James, who assures her she is just traumatized.

Cecilia believes that Adrian faked his death and that he is using his optics expertise to become invisible in order to torment her. She then works on how to prove that she’s being haunted by someone she can’t see.
